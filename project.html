Our Project

Designing a synthetic biology solution for PCOS hyperandrogenism

The Problem

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) affects millions worldwide and is often associated with elevated androgen levels. Current treatments are limited, non-specific, and may have significant side effects.

Our project focuses on addressing hyperandrogenism at its source using a targeted and biologically engineered solution.

Background & Motivation

Synthetic biology offers the potential to design living systems that can sense and respond to disease markers. By leveraging engineered cells, we aim to create a dynamic therapeutic system rather than a static treatment.

Our Design

We are developing a genetically engineered system capable of detecting elevated androgen levels and responding by modulating hormone activity.

  • Host organism selection and safety considerations
  • Design of a responsive genetic circuit
  • Controlled expression of therapeutic components
